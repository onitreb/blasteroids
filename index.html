Blasteroids

You don’t shoot the little rocks — you collect them. Fly near small asteroids to attract them into your magnet ring, then burst them outward to smash big ones.

Fly
W / ↑ thrust • A,D / ←,→ rotate • S / ↓ brake
Magnet + Burst
Space or Left Click to burst attached rocks outward
Rules
Fast Small→(Med/Large) fractures it • otherwise everything bounces
Utility
F fullscreen • R restart
Tip: keep your forcefield ring just grazing the small rocks.
